Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026

Summary: TreasureSpotter does not collect, store or sell your personal data. Your API key and scan history stay on your device only. Camera images are sent directly to Anthropic's AI for analysis and are not stored by us.

1. Introduction

TreasureSpotter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use TreasureSpotter on your Android device.

2. Information We Collect

TreasureSpotter collects minimal information:

Camera Images: When you tap Scan Now, the app captures a single image frame from your camera. This image is sent directly to the Anthropic Claude AI API for analysis. Images are not stored on our servers and are not retained after analysis.

API Key: Your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party other than Anthropic's API.

Scan History: Your scan results are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us.

3. How We Use Information

Camera images are used solely to provide the treasure identification service. Images are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API and are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy (anthropic.com/privacy). We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the scanning service.

4. Data Storage

All data (API key, scan history, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. You can clear all app data at any time through the app's Settings menu or your device's app settings.

5. Permissions

Camera: Required to capture images for scanning. Only active when the app is open.

Internet: Required to send images to the Anthropic AI API for analysis.

6. Third Party Services

TreasureSpotter uses the Anthropic Claude AI API to analyse images. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

7. Children's Privacy

TreasureSpotter is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository at github.com/window369/Treasure-Spotter-
